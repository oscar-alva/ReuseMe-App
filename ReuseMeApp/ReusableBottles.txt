Did you know that in the 2020 International Coastal Cleanup annual event, over 600,000 plastic bottles were picked up from coasts around the world? And that's just the ones that were found. The actual number of plastic bottles that end up in our ocean is unknown but likely unimaginable. Plastic bottles are among the top 5 items of waste that end up in our oceans.

(source: https://oceanconservancy.org )
