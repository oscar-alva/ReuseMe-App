Did you know that in the 2020 International Coastal Cleanup annual event, among the top 10 items of waste that were found in our coasts around the world were food wrappers and take out containers? Developing a habit to bring food from home instead of purchasing food outside can have a tremendous impact in lowering the amount of this kind of waste that continues to pollute our oceans.

(source: https://oceanconservancy.org )
